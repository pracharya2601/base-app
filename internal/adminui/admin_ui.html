Ops command

        
Tell the platform what to do in plain English — e.g. "add a blog_posts collection with title, body, and a relation to authors, rbac on", "create a support-readonly role that can read orders and support_tickets", or "make alice@example.com a support-readonly user". The ops agent drafts the change (schema, role, role assignment, or seed records) and queues it for your approval — nothing is applied until you approve it below in Tasks.
